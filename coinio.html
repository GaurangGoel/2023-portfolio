Product Design

Coinio

  • Timeline
    2 weeks
  • Role
    Product Designer
  • Tools Used
    Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
Made with

Overview

Coinio is a cryptocurrency mobile app designed to make navigating the world of cryptocurrencies easier for beginners. The primary objective of this project was to create an intuitive and seamless user experience that helps users understand and manage their crypto investments with ease. This case study will walk you through my design process, highlighting key decisions and showcasing the skills I used to create this app.

Personal Connection

My fascination with cryptocurrencies led me to notice that many beginners, including my friend, were overwhelmed by the complexity of existing apps. Inspired by his struggles, I decided to create Coinio, a user-friendly solution that simplifies the cryptocurrency experience, empowering beginners to manage their digital assets with confidence.

Understanding the Challenges

  • Market analysis: I started by studied the features, user interfaces, and overall user experiences of existing cryptocurrency apps to understand the current landscape and determine potential areas of improvement.

  • User interviews: I conducted one-on-one interviews with potential users who had varying levels of experience with cryptocurrencies to understand their motivations, challenges, and expectations when using cryptocurrency apps.

  • Surveys: I created and distributed online surveys to gather quantitative data on user preferences, frustrations, and desired features. This data helped to supplement the qualitative insights from the interviews and provided a broader understanding of the target audience's needs.

Findings from Research

  • Overwhelming complexity: Many beginners found existing cryptocurrency apps to be complex and difficult to navigate. They often felt overwhelmed by the sheer amount of information, features, and jargon, which created barriers to entry for newcomers.

  • Desire for a simplified interface: Beginners in the cryptocurrency space wanted an app with a clean, simple, and intuitive interface that made it easy to access the most important features and information.

  • Personalized experience: Users expressed interest in having a personalized experience that catered to their specific needs and preferences.

Problem Statement

New users often find it difficult to understand and navigate cryptocurrency apps, which can lead to frustration and reduced adoption of these platforms. To address this issue, I aimed to create a seamless and intuitive user experience that caters to beginners without compromising on advanced features for experienced users.

Onboarding & Verification

To make the onboarding process as simple as possible, I designed a step-by-step walkthrough with clear instructions.

Home & Markets

The home screen was designed to provide an overview of the user's portfolio and quick access to the most important features of the app. Users can view real-time data for various cryptocurrencies, add them to their watchlist, or invest in them via the Markets tab.

Buy, Sell & Quick Trade Cryptocurrency

In addition to the standard buy and sell options, the app also features a quick trade functionality for users who want to make transactions swiftly, allowing users to rapidly exchange their cryptocurrencies with minimal effort, making the trading process even more accessible and efficient.

Custom Icons & Illustrations

To enhance the visual appeal and create a unique identity for Coinio, I designed custom icons and illustrations tailored to the app's needs. These visual elements not only help users easily recognize and navigate different features of the app, but also contribute to a consistent and engaging user experience.

Key Learnings

  • Importance of simplicity: I learned how breaking down complex tasks into smaller, manageable steps to help users feel confident in managing their cryptocurrency investments.

  • Iterative design process: By creating multiple mockups and receiving feedback from potential users, I was able to make informed decisions and improve the overall design.

  • Balancing aesthetics and functionality: I reinforced the need to find a balance between creating a visually appealing app and ensuring that it is user-friendly and easy to navigate.
