About Me
Made with

Hello there! 👋🏻 I'm Gaurang Goel, a product designer and a senior Computer Science student at the University of Texas at Dallas. I'm passionate about crafting visually appealing and functional digital experiences that enhance users' lives.

My recent experience includes interning with Nutanix, a leading enterprise cloud computing company, where I collaborated with cross-functional teams to deliver seamless and user-friendly interface designs for cloud storage solutions. I was involved in conducting user research, creating wireframes, and validating designs through usability testing.

Before joining Nutanix, I worked as an intern at Symbotic, working alongside some of the industry's most gifted designers. At Symbotic, I was given the opportunity to explore various aspects of design and contribute to real-world projects that enhanced my understanding of user needs and business objectives.

In the summer of 2022, I also served as a Graphic Designer Intern at Corsair. During my time there, I teamed up with the creative minds at Gamer Sensei to develop eye-catching visuals for social media campaigns. This experience taught me the importance of integrating brand identity into design, as well as how to manage multiple projects in a fast-paced environment.

When I'm not immersed in design projects, I enjoy experimenting with innovative interaction designs, delving into the exciting world of machine learning and artificial intelligence, and creating engaging content that truly resonates. In my downtime, you might catch me hitting the squash court.

At the core, I believe that design is about improving people's lives. This drives my commitment to developing solutions that not only possess aesthetic appeal but also address real-world challenges and elevate user experiences.

If you're curious to know more about me or my work, please don't hesitate to reach out!
