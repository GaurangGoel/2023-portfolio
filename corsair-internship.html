Graphic Design Internship at Corsair

  • Timeline
    May - August 2022
  • Role
    Graphic Designer
  • Tools Used
    Adobe Creative Suite, Figma
Made with

Overview

During my summer internship at Corsair in 2022, I had the privilege of working with the talented team at Gamer Sensei, Corsair's acquisition. Throughout this experience, I was involved in various graphic design projects, primarily focusing on creating social media content. Additionally, I worked on a UX project that significantly contributed to my professional growth. Although I cannot share the details of the UX project publicly, I am excited to showcase my graphic design work and share the valuable lessons I learned during my internship.

Digital Content Creation

The primary goal of this project was to design visually appealing and engaging graphics for Gamer Sensei's social media channels, increasing brand awareness and user engagement.

Research and Analysis

I began by conducting an in-depth analysis of the existing social media content and studying successful examples within the gaming industry. This research helped me identify trends and best practices that could be applied to Gamer Sensei's content strategy.

Ideation and Collaboration

Working alongside the marketing and design teams, I participated in brainstorming sessions, exploring various concepts and design directions for social media posts. We iterated on several ideas, refining them based on feedback from stakeholders and performance metrics.

Final Design and Execution

Once the concepts were approved, I developed the final graphics using design tools like Adobe Creative Suite. I focused on creating a consistent visual language across all social media channels while ensuring each graphic captured the essence of the message and catered to the target audience.

Performance Evaluation and Iteration

After the graphics were published, I worked with the marketing team to analyze the performance of the content. We used key metrics such as engagement, reach, and impressions to identify areas of improvement and iterate on the designs accordingly.

Key Learnings

  • The importance of a data-driven approach to design, using performance metrics to inform decisions and iterate on designs.
  • Collaboration and communication with cross-functional teams, ensuring that designs align with overall business goals and objectives.
  • The ability to work under tight deadlines and manage multiple projects simultaneously.
  • Adapting to different design styles while maintaining brand consistency.

My internship at Corsair, working with Gamer Sensei, provided me with invaluable experience and insights into the world of graphic design and user experience. I am eager to apply these skills to my future endeavors as a Product Designer.

If you would like to learn more about my UX project and internship experience, please reach out to me!
