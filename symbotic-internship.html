UX Design Internship at Symbotic

  • Timeline
    January - June 2023
  • Role
    UX Designer
  • Tools Used
    Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
Made with

Overview

As a UX Designer Intern at Symbotic, I had the unique opportunity to work on cutting-edge projects, shaping the user experience for new products in their early stages of development. Although I am unable to share specific details about these projects due to a non-disclosure agreement (NDA), I am excited to share my journey and the valuable skills I gained throughout my internship.

Understanding the Challenge

One of the projects I worked on during my internship involved a new product with limited user adoption and rapidly changing requirements. This product was in its infancy, and my task was to design a user experience that would cater to the evolving needs of its users while keeping stakeholders' goals in mind.

Research and Empathy

To deeply understand the user needs and pain points, I conducted extensive user research, including interviews, surveys, and observation. I also visited the site to experience the product firsthand, which was crucial in building empathy and understanding the context in which the product was used.

Ideation and Prototyping

Based on the insights from my research, I developed user flows and wireframes, taking into account the different use cases and scenarios. I collaborated with cross-functional teams, ensuring that my designs were feasible and aligned with the company's vision. Using rapid prototyping techniques, I iterated on the designs and gathered feedback from users and stakeholders.

Testing and Validation

To validate my designs, I conducted usability testing sessions with real users, carefully analyzing their interactions and feedback. I used this data to refine my design solutions, ensuring they met user needs and were easy to navigate.

Iteration and Improvement

After each round of testing and validation, I iterated on the designs to further enhance the user experience. By closely collaborating with the development team, we were able to implement changes quickly and efficiently, resulting in a product that was not only user-friendly but also scalable and adaptable to evolving requirements.

Throughout my internship, I gained invaluable experience in adapting to a rapidly changing product landscape, learning how to balance user needs with stakeholder goals. I honed my skills in user research, prototyping, and validation, while also learning the importance of cross-functional collaboration and communication.

While I'm unable to share specific project details publicly, I believe my journey demonstrates my ability to navigate complex challenges and create meaningful user experiences.

If you are interested in learning more about my internship experience, please feel free to reach out to me. I'd be more than happy to discuss my accomplishments.
